The Air

On the northern slopes of Fruška Gora, where forest air from the national park meets the river breezes of the Danube, the air stays clean and oxygen-rich through every season — above the winter haze that settles over the city below. The Institutes of Sremska Kamenica — among South-East Europe's leading cardiology and pulmonology centres — were established nearby generations ago: an independent testament to the quality of the air here.

Flagship Development

On the northern slopes of Fruška Gora, in Sremska Kamenica, Tatar Hill stands at the highest residential point near Novi Sad. Two boutique buildings. Twelve apartments.
